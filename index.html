Hi, I'm a developer. I love clean designs & elegant code.

    A little bit about me.

    My home base is in Portland, Oregon. My journey into tech started with my curiosity for how websites worked and to discover the behind the scenes look at websites. I was fascinated with how easily I could change the appearance of a webpage with just a few lines of CSS. Ever since I've been hooked and wanted to learn more about the ins and outs of coding.

    I'm a recent graduate of the Fullstack Flex bootcamp at Thinkful. I've found by combining my natural curiosity and passion for problem solving, my love for programming continues to grow. I can use my instinct for design and love for collaborating with others, to provide myself the creative freedom and versatility I've been looking for.

    Hike-IT!

    One of the best parts about living in the PNW is how incredibly beautiful the scenery is. I enjoy getting outside and exploring hiking trails near-by or afar. I created this responsive web app to provide users with a quick reference to trails based on the city and state they choose. Users are given the location, star rating, length and link for more information to each trail. Let the adventures begin!

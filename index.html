Hi, I'm a developer. I love clean designs & elegant code.

    A little bit about me.

    My home base is in Portland, Oregon. My journey into tech started with my curiosity to learn behind the scenes of how websites work. I was fascinated with how easily I could change the appearance of a webpage with just a few lines of CSS. Ever since I've been hooked and wanted to learn more about the ins and outs of coding.

    I've found by combining my natural curiosity and passion for problem solving, my love for programming continues to grow. I can use my instinct for design and love for collaborating with others, to provide myself the creative freedom and versatility I've been looking for. In my free time, I love exploring my own backyard, the beautiful Pacific Northwest.
